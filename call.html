CRACS-17

National Conference On Current Research Advances in Computer Science

(17th & 18th March, 2017)

CALL FOR PAPERS

Prospective authors are invited to submit full length original manuscripts limited to 6 pages (which have not been submitted or published elsewhere in other conferences / journals) in single column, double line spacing, 10-point font in A4 size paper. Submissions shall include title, abstract, keywords, author and affiliation with email address.

TOPICS:

• Artificial intelligence
• Big data
• Cloud computing
• Data mining
• Grid computing
• Image processing
• Mobile computing
• Network security
• Parallel and distributed system
• Soft computing
• Software Engineering
• Wireless and sensor networks and any other topic related to the discipline
