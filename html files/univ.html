ABOUT THE UNIVERSITY

Kakatiya University was established in 19th August, 1976 to fulfill the aspirations of the Telangana people for higher education. The University was initially accredited with A grade. The University is situated in a sprawling area of about 650 acres. The University is vibrant with research activities carrying out the projects sponsored by various funding agencies. It has a spacious Central Library with a rich collection of books and research journals. Our university crossed the milestone of thirty one years and is poised to achieve greater academic excellence with dedication and commitment in the years to come. The artistic beauty of the temples with excellent sculpture of unique style, wild life sanctuaries, and the tanks with sparkling waters reflect nature's beauty and provide fabulous fete to the tourists.
